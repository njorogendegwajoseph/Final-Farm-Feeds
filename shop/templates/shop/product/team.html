Our Team

Mr David Murumba

Production Manager

David Murumba is an Animal Science graduate of Egerton University with over 35 years of experience in commercial and research livestock production systems. With a passion for Animal nutrition, David has previously worked for KALRO-Naivasha, Eatec Ltd (East Africa Tanning and Extra Company), Farmers Choice Ltd and Millennium Millers amongst others. He joined Economy Farm Products Ltd in 2006. He heads the Production department with key responsibilities in Feed formulation, production planning, training and quality assurance. Besides the basic training in animal science, David has over time earned certificates in Food/Feed Safety (HACCP), Feed Production and Formulation, and, Good Manufacturing Practices (GPM & QAS) systems. He is hands-on in the application of modern Least Cost Formulation (LCF) software in the production of quality animal feeds.
