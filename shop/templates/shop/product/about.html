About Farm Feeds

We strive to bring you the best in every produce, a promise we made from inception of this company. Below is the journey!

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Economy Farm products (k) ltd has been providing your livestock with well balanced quality feeds and distributed animal health products for maximum returns since 1998. Ever since then, providing the farmer with the necessary tools for health, high yielding livestock has been our main objective. Over the last 25 years, we have continued to grow in order to support you more efficiently and effectively.

  • Market Establishment

    We have established a wide market network with 4 established regions to serve you more efficiently. Region 1 is Nairobi and its environs; Region 2 is the Mt. Kenya region which includes areas within Eastern and Central province; Region 3 which covers Eldoret, Kitale, Naivasha and Nakuru is the North Rift region and The Western Region which covers Kakamega, Bungoma, Webuye and Kiminini. Each of these regions have designated sales people to ensure the region’s supply needs are met and distributors are promptly restocked.

  • Transition to Full Service

    To further demonstrate our desire for your farms to be successful, we have also provided avenues for each farmer to remain up-to-date on the latest farming practices. Through our well trained technical personnel, we provide technical follow-ups, seminars and field days. Be sure to check the website’s bulletin board to remain updated on the next training within your region. Farm Feeds currently has a labour force of 35 permanent employees and 70 casual. The work force is tasked with ensuring our state of the art go down is running smoothly and consistently producing the high quality of the feeds that you have come to expect and appreciate. The employees are regularly trained in the latest first aid and fire fighting techniques. We also have numerous delivery vans for the efficient delivery to the distributors across the 4 regions.

  • Service_Oriented

    We work tirelessly to ensure that each farmer we interact with feels heard and their needs are responded to in a timely manner. We always strives to maintain the highest integrity within our sector. Moreover, we work to ensure that the customers’ needs are met to ensure that farmers maximise their production and achieve higher returns.
